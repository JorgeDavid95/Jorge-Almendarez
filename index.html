Intro

What I am all about.

I'm Jorge Almendarez, 22 years old guy who loves sharing his vision of the world through a camera lens. Grew and born at Tegucigalpa, Honduras, with a family of 4. Filmed everything since I was 15 years old, not great material but it was something. Studied Comunication & Adverting at UNITEC. Place in which labor opportunities pop up by making classmates some project favors.

Love indie music (some oldies too), hangout with close friends, enjoy the view from the window of my car, and the small details of someone smilling. I think you will get to know me better by looking to some of my works.
